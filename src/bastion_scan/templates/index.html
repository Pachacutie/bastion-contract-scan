Upload your contract. See what's hiding in the fine print.

What is this? A free tool that reads your contract and flags the clauses that could cost you money, limit your rights, or lock you in. Built by someone with a decade of experience in the home security industry — but it works on any contract.
  • Upload a PDF or paste text from any contract — security, telecom, gym, SaaS, anything
  • Get a plain-English report with color-coded flags: red (serious), yellow (watch out), green (just the facts)
  • No account, no signup, no cost — just answers
